# 🛠️ Step 3: Installing CFW (Evilnat)

---

## 🔌 1. Restart the console

- Fully turn off your PS3  
- Turn it back on  

---

## 💻 2. Prepare the USB drive on PC

1. Plug your USB drive into your computer  
2. Open the USB drive  
3. Find the file: **dump.bin**  
4. Copy it to your PC (keep it safe)  

📌 This file is a **backup of your flash memory** (very important in case of issues)

---

## 📥 3. Download CFW (Evilnat)

👉 On the guide’s GitHub, go to the **Software** folder  

You will see a file called **Liens.md**. Click it, then click the link that matches your system version.

### 📌 Choose the correct version:

- **4.88** → if your PS3 is between **4.85 and 4.88**  
- **4.92** → if your PS3 is between **4.90 and 4.92**  

---

## 📂 4. Put the files on the USB drive

In the downloaded folder, you will have:

PS3/  
└── UPDATE/  
  └── PS3UPDAT.PUP  

👉 Copy the entire **PS3 folder** to your USB drive  

✔️ Expected structure on the USB:  
└── PS3/  
  └── UPDATE/  
    └── PS3UPDAT.PUP  

---

## 🔌 5. Plug the USB into the PS3

- Plug the USB into the **rightmost port**  
  (closest to the disc drive)

---

## ⚙️ 6. Install the CFW update

1. Go to: Settings → System Update  
2. Choose: **Update via Storage Media**  
3. If everything is correct, you will see: Version: Evilnat (PEX or similar)  

👉 If you see this = ✅ perfect  

---

## ▶️ 7. Start the installation

- Click **OK**  
- Accept all conditions  
- Wait for the installation to finish  

⚠️ Do not touch anything during the update  

---

## 🎮 8. Verify that the jailbreak worked

After reboot:

- The **PlayStation 3** logo may be replaced with **Evilnat**  

👉 If yes → 🎉 **jailbreak successful!**

---

# 🎉 Congratulations!

Your PS3 is now running **CFW**
