# ✅ PS3 Models Compatible with CFW (Custom Firmware)

## 🟢 1. PS3 FAT (original “fat” models)

👉 **ALL FAT models are compatible with CFW**

### Models:
- CECHAxx  
- CECHBxx  
- CECHCxx  
- CECHExx  
- CECHGxx to CECHQxx  

✔️ 100% compatible  
✔️ Firmware version does not matter  

---

## 🟢 2. PS3 Slim (CFW compatible)

👉 **100% compatible:**
- CECH-20XX  
- CECH-21XX  

✔️ Always compatible with CFW  

---

## 🟡 3. PS3 Slim (partially compatible)

👉 **CECH-25XX (WARNING ⚠️)**

✔️ Compatible **ONLY IF:**
- Minimum firmware ≤ 3.56  
- Manufactured before April 2011  
- Date code: 9 / 0 / 1A  

❌ **Not compatible if:**
- MinVerChk = 3.60+  

📌 So → you must check before  

---
