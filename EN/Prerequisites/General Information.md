# 📌 General Information

Many people say that to jailbreak a console, it is important to have the correct firmware version.  
To be honest, no: as long as your console model is compatible with CFW, the firmware version doesn’t really matter — it will work the same.

---

## 🔑 USB Drive

When plugging your USB drive into your PlayStation 3, make sure to use the **rightmost USB port** (the one closest to the disc drive).  
Otherwise, the console may not detect the USB drive.

---

## 🔄 Reset

If you bought a PlayStation 3 or have one that hasn’t been used for a long time, I strongly recommend **resetting it**.

👉 Why?  
To avoid bugs or issues.

---

## ⬆️ Firmware Update

If your console is running an outdated firmware, I recommend **updating it**:

- ✔️ Better internet performance  
- ✔️ Fewer issues with CFW  
- ✔️ Latest version: **4.92**

---

## 🌐 Internet Connection

To download games faster or improve internet access on your PS3, I recommend using an **Ethernet cable** connected to the back of the console.

👉 Benefits:
- Better connection speed  
- Fewer browser issues  

---

## 💾 Storage (Hard Drive)

Older PS3 models often don’t have enough storage space.

👉 I recommend upgrading your hard drive:
- 256 GB  
- 512 GB  
- 1 TB  

📌 **Important:**  
If you jailbreak your console and then replace the hard drive, you will need to **redo the jailbreak** after installing the new drive.

👉 However:
- It will be faster than the first time  
- The Memory Patch is not stored on the hard drive, but directly on the console’s motherboard  

---

## ⚠️ PSN (Very important)

❗ Never log into PSN with a jailbroken PS3.

👉 Risks:
- ❌ Console ban  
- ❌ PSN account ban
